About

Khalili is an innovative app that assists the visually impaired and blind by identifying objects and providing voice interactions.

Exclusive Features

Khalili offers a unique digital space that brings together pharmacists in Algeria for the purpose of medication exchange and collaboration.

Social Impact

Khalili facilitates communication and assistance between visually impaired individuals and their caregivers.

Security Impact

The app ensures safe usage of items, preventing potential misuse of expired products.

Environmental Impact

Proper identification and disposal of expired items help preserve the environment.

Financial Impact

Avoiding wastage of items improves financial performance and conserves public resources.

Daily life Impact

Khalili helps the visually impaired quickly identify medications and other essential items.

Object Identification

Khalili allows users to identify objects in their surroundings using the device's camera. The app provides audible descriptions of the identified objects, making it easier for visually impaired users to understand their environment.

Voice Commands

Khalili supports voice commands, allowing users to interact with the app hands-free. Users can issue commands to identify objects, get information about their surroundings, and control other app functionalities.

Text-to-Speech

Khalili uses advanced text-to-speech technology to provide audible feedback to the users. This feature reads out the names and descriptions of identified objects, ensuring users receive clear and understandable information.

Profile and Preferences Management

The "Profile" page allows users to manage their settings and preferences. Users can customize their experience, update their information, and control how the app interacts with them.

Location Services

Khalili integrates with Google Maps to provide location-based services. Users can receive audible directions, locate nearby places of interest, and navigate their surroundings with greater ease.
